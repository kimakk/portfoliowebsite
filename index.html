Film works by Kim Akkermans

Letter of a Tree

If a tree could speak, what would it say to the world?
In a project about creating a new design language, I researched sustainable ways
of creating paper and how it can ecologically-friendly decompose in nature.

A Childhood Dream

The overall topic for this film was supposed to be about a hut.
I remember a hut in my childhood playground and started to film some shots there.
When I started editing, I realised something was missing. It was the state of this world
and the children whose childhood and dreams are violently taken from them.
In this way, I wanted to show the contrast between these two worlds.

Haagse Trots

The Hague has been my home for the past three years now, and I've noticed a certain
type of pride for this city. The mullet has become a statement and is said to be from
The Hague, so a "Hague Mullet Atlas" has been published for example. This is one part
of the film where I research and dive into stories, people and buildings
that remind me why this certain pride exists.

Alter Ego

I like to give my archives a meaning, so when I had to create a project about an alter ego,
I came across an old voice memo, and started building a "world" around it
of an alter ego who is still the same person but is liberated from
the insecurities of an younger version of themselves.
